Introduction

WRITE is a student-focused academic support platform designed to help college students manage assignment pressure in a smarter and more organized way. With increasing workload and tight deadlines, WRITE provides a reliable and customizable solution for neat and timely assignment submission.

Problem Statement

  • Students face time constraints due to exams, projects, and internships.
  • Existing assignment services are unorganized and unreliable.
  • No transparency in pricing, deadlines, or customization.
  • Stress, poor presentation, and missed deadlines are common.

Proposed Solution

WRITE offers a centralized web-based platform where students can get their assignments completed neatly and professionally according to their exact requirements, ensuring quality presentation and timely delivery.

Key Features

Handwriting Styles

Multiple handwriting options for authentic presentation.

Pen Selection

Blue, black, gel pens as per requirement.

Deadline Based Pricing

Fair pricing for normal and urgent submissions.

Clear Diagrams

Neat and accurate diagrams for better evaluation.

Transparent System

No hidden charges or confusion.

Professional Workflow

Systematic and reliable process from order to delivery.

Vision

The long-term vision of WRITE is to become a trusted academic support platform. Starting with handwritten assignments, WRITE aims to expand into notes preparation, lab records, project documentation, and academic formatting services.
